<- R for GIS & Remote Sensing

Transform R into a Geospatial Powerhouse

Join our 3-week virtual mentorship program and learn how to use R for spatial data analysis, remote sensing, cartography, and geovisualization.

3 Weeks Starts: TBA Online (Live + Assignments) 25 Mentees

🧭 What You'll Learn

1
Foundations of R
  • R & RStudio setup
  • R basics & spatial data types
  • Coordinate Reference Systems (CRS)
  • Basic mapping with ggplot2 & sf
2
Vector Data Analysis
  • Spatial joins, buffers, clipping
  • Attribute queries & thematic mapping with tmap
3
Raster & Remote Sensing
  • Working with satellite imagery
  • NDVI & other spectral indices
  • Image classification & time series
  • Google Earth Engine in R
4
Cartography & Visualization
  • Map styling, layout & exporting
  • Interactive maps with leaflet
5
Capstone & Career
  • Final project presentations
  • Publishing maps & dashboards
  • Career guidance in geospatial data science
